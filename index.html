Module 01

Das Bankett der Kaiserin

Das Abendmahl

A pâté worthy of her beauty is served each evening. Stir it gently in its crystal vessel. A warmed meal, reminiscent of the heat of a fresh hunt, delights her greatly. For the prosperity of the empire, add 1/8 tsp of Lysine.

Trockene Gaben

The supply of dry delicacies is replenished by unseen magic, yet your watchful eye must ensure it never dwindles. A weekly polishing of her serving dish is essential.

Nektar der Götter

Churu: Offer this ambrosia as a token of your deepest adoration, especially after the trying rituals of grooming.

Der Lebensbrunnen

The water must possess the purity of a mountain spring. It must be renewed every two days, for only the freshest offering is worthy of the Empress.

Module 02

Imperial Diversions

A soul of such sensitivity and beauty requires moments of whim and play. It is your sacred duty to delight Her Majesty.

  • Die Jagd nach dem Sternenlicht

    Capture a beam of light at dusk and let it dance like a firefly through her chambers.

  • Spiele der Musen

    Let small feathers and shining orbs bounce from the furniture like notes in a symphony.

Module 03

Die Rituale der Schönheit

Der Kaiserliche Mantel

Die Fenster zur Seele

Her eyes, twin emeralds of startling clarity, must be free of any blemish. A gentle touch ensures her gaze continues to enchant.
